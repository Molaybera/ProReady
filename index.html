💡 Problem

We have discuss with our loacl players and coach and we plan to build the solution on the common problem they are facing.
_____
Local athletes struggle to manage proper match preparation and often lack visibility to coaches and scouts. At the same time, coaches rely on scattered social media highlights without structured performance data or consistency tracking.

🚀 Solution

ProReady is a sports performance and scouting platform that helps athletes prepare smarter through personalized match plans, readiness tracking, and performance analysis, while also giving them a structured network to showcase gameplay highlights and get discovered by coaches.

What's Next

🔮 Future Upgrades of ProReady

Future upgrades of ProReady include deeper AI integration for intelligent match preparation analysis, a calendar agent that automatically schedules training and recovery routines based on player availability, and smart reminder systems using notifications and AI voice calls to ensure athletes stay consistent with their preparation plans before important matches.
